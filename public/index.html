zoom.it

What is it?

zoom.it lets you publish & view your high-resolution images seamlessly and blazingly fast, no matter whether you are on a desktop computer, tablet, or mobile phone.

Play around with the demo above.

What’s the current status?

zoom.it is no longer part of Microsoft and has been revived as an open-source project.

We’re currently not converting new images, but existing images—and their pages and embeds—should work. We are working very hard to restore the service to its old glory and beyond!
