zoomhub

What is it?

zoomhub lets you share & view your high-resolution images seamlessly and blazingly fast, no matter whether you are on a desktop computer, tablet, or mobile phone.

Play around with the demo above and experience it yourself!

What’s the current status?

zoomhub is the open-source and spiritual successor of Microsoft’s zoom.it. It is developed and maintained by volunteers who were members of the original zoom.it team.

We’re currently not converting new images, but existing images—including their pages and embeds—should work. We are working very hard to restore the service to its old glory and beyond!
